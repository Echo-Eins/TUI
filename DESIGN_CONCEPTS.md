- Accent 1 (CPU): `#f38ba8` (Red)
- Accent 2 (GPU): `#94e2d5` (Cyan)
- Accent 3 (RAM): `#89b4fa` (Blue)
- Accent 4 (Disk): `#a6e3a1` (Green)
- Accent 5 (Network): `#f9e2af` (Yellow)
- Warning: `#fab387` (Orange)
- Error: `#f38ba8` (Red)
- Success: `#a6e3a1` (Green)

- Use for syntax highlighting and gradients in graphs
